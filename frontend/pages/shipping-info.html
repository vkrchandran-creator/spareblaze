Section 4 – Packaging Standards

Automotive parts are precision components — a dented brake caliper or a cracked sensor housing renders the part useless. We engineer our packaging to withstand the real-world conditions of India's logistics network, including rough handling, long-haul freight, and monsoon weather:

Multi-Layer Protection

Section 5 – Frequently Asked Shipping Questions

Can I change the delivery address after placing my order?
Address changes are possible only before the order is dispatched. Contact us immediately via email or phone with your Order ID and the corrected address. Once the AWB is generated and the order is handed over to our courier partner, address changes are not possible in transit. For future orders, save multiple delivery addresses under My Account to streamline the process.
What if I am not home when the delivery agent arrives?
The delivery agent will make up to 3 attempts on consecutive business days, calling you before each attempt. If all three attempts fail, the package is returned to our warehouse (marked RTO — Return to Origin). A refund for the item value (minus the COD handling fee for COD orders) is processed within 7–10 business days of the package reaching us. To reschedule a delivery attempt, contact the courier directly using the tracking number from your dispatch email.
Do you ship to APO/FPO military addresses or outside India?
SpareBlaze currently ships exclusively within India and does not support APO, FPO, or international shipping addresses. We cover all 28 states, 8 union territories, and over 25,000 PIN codes domestically. International shipping is on our development roadmap — register your interest by contacting us at support@spareblaze.com and we will notify you when it becomes available.
How are heavy or oversized parts like engine assemblies shipped?
Heavy parts (over 20 kg) and oversized components such as engine assemblies, gearboxes, full door panels, and large body panels are shipped via surface freight using specialised automotive logistics partners. These shipments are palletised or crated depending on the item's fragility. Freight charges are calculated at checkout based on the actual or volumetric weight (whichever is higher) and your delivery zone. Delivery for freight shipments takes an additional 2–3 business days beyond the standard timeline for your zone. Our team will call you to coordinate a convenient delivery window for large freight items.
