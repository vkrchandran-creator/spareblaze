30-Day Returns

Return any eligible item within 30 days of delivery — no questions asked on defective or incorrect items.

Full Refund

Get a complete refund to your original payment method with no deductions on qualifying returns.

Free Doorstep Pickup

We arrange a free courier pickup from your address for all approved returns.

Return Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for a return, all of the following conditions must be met at the time of your return request:

  • Within 30 days of delivery date — Returns initiated after 30 days of the confirmed delivery date will not be accepted.
  • Unused and uninstalled condition — The part must not have been fitted to or installed in any vehicle. Any fitment marks will void the return eligibility.
  • Original packaging, all accessories & documentation intact — Return in the original manufacturer packaging with all accessories, bolts, gaskets, and any documentation.
  • Valid Order ID or invoice provided — You must provide your SpareBlaze Order ID (format: SB-XXXXXXXXX) or a copy of the purchase invoice.
  • Photographic or video evidence for defective claims submitted within 48 hours of delivery — For items received damaged or defective, clear unboxing evidence must be sent to support@spareblaze.in within 48 hours.

Items Exempt from Returns

These items can only be returned if dead-on-arrival (DOA) with unboxing video proof
  • Electrical & electronic components — Sensors, ECUs, ignition modules, relays, and all electronic assemblies are non-returnable unless DOA with unboxing video.
  • Fluids, oils & lubricants with broken factory seal — Engine oils, brake fluids, coolants, and any liquid products with tampered or opened seals cannot be returned.
  • Final Sale / clearance items — Products purchased at clearance or final sale prices are not eligible for return or exchange unless defective.
  • Installed or used parts showing fitment marks — Any component with visible signs of installation, mounting scratches, torque marks, or fluid contact is non-returnable.
  • Custom or special-order parts sourced on request — Parts ordered specifically for your vehicle that are not part of regular inventory cannot be returned.
  • Perishable items: air filters, cabin filters, bulbs — These items are non-returnable once removed from their sealed manufacturer packaging.

How to Return — Step by Step

1

Initiate

Contact us within 30 days of delivery via email at returns@spareblaze.in or call 1800 123 4567. Have your Order ID ready.

2

Document

Share clear photographs or an unboxing video showing the defect, incorrect part, or damaged packaging with the return request.

3

Approval

Our returns team reviews your request in 24–48 business hours and sends you a Return Authorisation (RA) number via email.

4

Pickup

We schedule a free doorstep courier pickup within 2–3 business days. Pack the item securely with the RA number written on the box.

5

Inspection

Our warehouse team inspects the returned item within 3–5 business days of receipt to verify it meets all return eligibility criteria.

6

Refund

Refund is processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method once inspection approval is confirmed.

Exchanges & Replacements

In addition to refunds, SpareBlaze offers direct part replacement for items received in a defective condition or where an incorrect part was shipped due to our error. A replacement order will be raised at no additional cost once the original item's return is approved.

To request an exchange instead of a refund, mention this preference when you contact us to initiate the return. Our team will confirm availability of replacement stock before processing.

Exchanges are subject to the same eligibility criteria as standard returns. The replacement item will be dispatched using the same shipping method used for your original order.

Priority Dispatch for Immobilised Vehicles

If a defective part has left your vehicle undriveable, let us know when initiating the return. We will prioritise your replacement dispatch — typically within 1 business day — and assign a dedicated support agent to minimise your downtime.
